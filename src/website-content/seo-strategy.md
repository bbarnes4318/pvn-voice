## SEO Content Strategy

### Keyword Focus
- Primary focus on long-tail, solution-oriented keywords that match searcher intent
- Secondary focus on broader industry terms to establish authority
- Tertiary focus on location-based terms for businesses seeking local providers

### Content Types & Distribution
1. **Educational Blog Posts (60%)**
   - Comprehensive guides on business communication topics
   - How-to articles for setting up and optimizing VoIP systems
   - Industry trend analysis and predictions
   - Remote work communication best practices

2. **Case Studies & Success Stories (20%)**
   - Industry-specific implementation examples
   - Problem-solution narratives with measurable results
   - Customer testimonials with specific benefits highlighted

3. **Technical Resources (10%)**
   - Setup tutorials and guides
   - Troubleshooting resources
   - Integration walkthroughs
   - Advanced configuration tips

4. **Company News & Updates (10%)**
   - Product enhancement announcements
   - Industry event coverage
   - Company milestones and achievements
   - Team spotlights and expertise showcases

### Content Calendar Structure
- Publish 2-3 new blog posts per month (minimum 1,500 words each)
- Release 1 case study per month
- Quarterly publishing of comprehensive guides (3,000+ words)
- Regular updates to product and service pages to keep content fresh

## On-Page SEO Elements

### Title Tags
Format: Primary Keyword | Secondary Keyword | Brand Name
Example: "Cloud Business Phone System | VoIP Services for SMBs | PVN Dialer"
Length: 50-60 characters

### Meta Descriptions
Format: Action verb + value proposition + call to action
Example: "Transform your business communication with our cloud phone system. Get enterprise features at small business prices. Start your free trial today."
Length: 140-160 characters

### URL Structure
Format: domain.com/service-category/specific-service
Example: pvndialer.com/services/business-phone-system
- Keep URLs short and descriptive
- Use hyphens to separate words
- Include primary keyword when relevant
- Avoid parameter strings

### Heading Structure
- H1: Main page title (include primary keyword)
- H2: Major section headings (include secondary keywords)
- H3: Subsection headings (include tertiary keywords or variations)
- H4-H6: Minor section headings as needed

### Image Optimization
- Descriptive file names (e.g., business-voip-dashboard.jpg)
- Alt text including relevant keywords
- Compressed file sizes for fast loading
- Proper dimensions to avoid scaling
- Unique images when possible (avoid stock photos)

### Content Optimization
- Primary keyword in first 100 words
- Keyword variations throughout content
- Natural language and readability (aim for 8th-grade reading level)
- Bulleted lists and short paragraphs for scannability
- Internal links to relevant content (3-5 per page)
- External links to authoritative sources when appropriate

## Technical SEO Considerations

### Site Architecture
- Clear hierarchical structure
- Maximum 3 clicks to reach any page
- Logical site sections and categories
- Breadcrumb navigation on all pages

### Mobile Optimization
- Responsive design for all devices
- Touch-friendly navigation elements
- Readable text without zooming
- Properly spaced buttons and links
- Fast loading on mobile connections

### Page Speed Optimization
- Image compression and lazy loading
- Browser caching implementation
- Minified CSS and JavaScript
- Reduced server response time
- Prioritized visible content loading

### Structured Data
Implement schema markup for:
- Organization
- Service
- FAQ
- Review
- BreadcrumbList
- Article (for blog posts)
- VideoObject (for video content)
- Person (for team members)

### XML Sitemap
- Comprehensive inclusion of all indexable pages
- Regular updates when new content is published
- Proper prioritization of important pages
- Exclusion of non-canonical pages

### Robots.txt
- Allow crawling of all public content
- Block admin areas and duplicate content paths
- Block faceted navigation pages
- Include sitemap location
